And no, this isn't a joke.

Pittsburgh weather is unpredictable. It could start raining, hailing, or snowing any minute. As a student at Carnegie Mellon, it can be difficult to stay dry when walking between classes or just hanging out on The Cut. That's why we're launching the Plaid Umbrella Project to make umbrellas accessible to everyone on campus for free. Our goal is to install a network of umbrella dispensers so when the torrential downpour begins, you just swipe your ID card and grab an umbrella. Return it the next day. It's that simple.


Free

Swipe your ID card and pick up an umbrella. No fees.

Convenient

We'll put umbrella dispensers in easy-to-access locations.

Quality

Plaid umbrellas that are big enough to keep you dry.

Student-Run

Managed and developed by students just like you.
